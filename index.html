How we met

In August 2018, Justin accepted a career move that would send him packing from the DC area to Seattle with two suitcases and a lot of hope for a great new adventure. He was living the dream working for a large tech company and sharing an apartment in the trendiest part of town with his new best pal, Smokey. Only one thing was missing from his life, and as fate would have it he would soon find out just what a great new adventure he was in for. Less than one year later, a new neighbor was moving in down the hall from Justin. She had everything she needed to start her new life, except for a screwdriver to hang her shelves with. Karina had also just moved from Washington DC to work for a large tech company, and had also kept her bags packed small. So small, in fact, that her toolbox never made it to the West Coast with her. Killing two birds with one stone, Karina set out to find a neighbor with tools that she could borrow and to also make a new friend. With a bottle of wine in her hand and optimism in her heart, Karina stepped out of her apartment with a plan to knock on the first door she saw, except she was found and tackled by Smokey before she could begin her search! Her shelves were never hung, but the bottle of wine was enjoyed together with Justin and the rest is history. We can't wait to celebrate with all of you as we make incredible new memories that continue our great story.
